About Happy Dog Hotel

Happy Dog Hotel is committed to delivering the highest level of pet pampering, grooming, and training for dogs. From socialization to one-on-one interaction, luxury suites, Happy Dog Hotel will surpass your expectations.

All dogs that enter our facility are treated as if they are our own, allowing you to have peace of mind while you are away from your pet.
