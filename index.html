Happy Dog Hotel is the Ultimate Stay & Play Resort

Happy Dog hotel is the ultimate boarding and daycare destination for dogs. Our luxury pet resort is open 24/7 every day of the year and is conveniently nestled just off the main freeway. We feature an array of services and amenities that can be completely customized to your unique pet.
